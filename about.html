About Me

Marketer. Builder.
One person.

I'm TJ Marek — a full-funnel digital marketer and web designer based in Austin, TX. For the last 4.5 years I've operated as a one-person marketing department: building the strategy, running the campaigns, designing the pages, and owning every metric in between.

Most marketers hand off to a developer. Most developers don't think about conversion. I do both — which means the ads I write send traffic to pages I built, tracked with events I configured, feeding data into dashboards I maintain. Nothing gets lost in translation because there's no translation.

I hold a Full-Stack Web Development certificate from UT Austin's Coding Boot Camp and certifications from Google in Ads, Analytics, and more. I'm actively looking for a full-time role where I can bring that same end-to-end ownership to a team that values both sides of the equation.

How I Work

The principles I
refuse to compromise.

01
If it can't be measured, it doesn't count

Every campaign, every page, every email has a trackable goal. I don't do vanity metrics and I don't guess at results.

02
Own the whole funnel

Great ads deserve great landing pages. Great pages deserve great tracking. I think end-to-end and build end-to-end.

03
Ship fast, iterate faster

Done beats perfect. I launch, I measure, I improve. Every campaign is a test. Every test teaches something.

04
Design is a business tool

Good design isn't decoration — it's the difference between a visitor and a lead. I build with conversion as the first priority.
