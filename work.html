The Process

How every project
gets done right.

Step 01
Audit & Diagnose

Before touching anything, I audit what exists — campaigns, site performance, tracking setup, and competitive landscape. Problems identified before solutions proposed.

Step 02
Strategy & Plan

A clear plan with defined KPIs, realistic timelines, and a full-funnel view. Every tactic connects back to a measurable business outcome — no activity for activity's sake.

Step 03
Build & Launch

Campaigns configured, pages built, tracking verified. I don't hand off to a developer or wait for approvals — I build and ship, then measure from day one.

Step 04
Measure & Improve

Everything gets reviewed weekly. What's working gets scaled, what isn't gets cut or tested differently. The work isn't done at launch — it's done when the numbers say so.
